Axalta Coating Systems, a global leading coating company with more than 150 years of innovation heritage, has been doing business in India since 1996. A strategic market for Axalta in the Asia Pacific and it has been investing in India the last two decades. While Axalta continues to invest in technological innovation and manufacturing facilities in India, they also believe in investing in the education of the future leaders who will continue their legacy in innovation for the coating industry. We, at IIT Delhi, proudly bring to you, Axalta’s Campus Talk, an educational program designed to unleash the potential of future leaders and R&D enthusiasts and support them in their pursuits in materials science, chemical engineering, physics dynamism, and color technology – helping them to be real world-aware and work-ready.
